About

The Picture Lounge is a successful photographic gallery concept founded by Wanaka based Graphic Designer & Professional Photographer Christopher Thompson in 2011.

The gallery was created to allow some of New Zealand’s finest professional photographers to have a retail showcase for their work. Traditionally, many had struggled to get their work into fine art galleries, even though the work is undoubtedly deserving of such representation.

Due to the gallery’s location in the beautiful resort town of Wanaka, the focus is predominantly on artistic Central Otago regional landscapes, though the collection is made all the more exciting for the unique photography that captures the broader essence of ‘the New Zealand experience’ and also features travel and street photography from well beyond New Zealand’s borders.
