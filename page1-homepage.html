About


The Picture Lounge is a successful photographic gallery concept founded by Wanaka based Graphic Designer & Professional Photographer Christopher Thompson in 2011.

Onsite Christopher operates his graphic design business; hook. Established in 2000, this creative studio has produced all manner of high-end graphic communications for a range of New Zealand and International clients
