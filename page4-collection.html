TThese gorgeous hard-cover photographic books represent a cross-section of Wanaka based professional Photographer and Graphic Designer Christopher Thompson’s best and favourite images of his hometown, and the wider Central Otago, Fiordland, and Mackenzie districts in the South Island of New Zealand. The images range from the classically pictorial to the beautifully minimalist abstract works he is well known for. It is a wonderful collection of his artistic photography – showcasing the stunning landscapes of New Zealand.

The books can be purchased direct from the gallery (artist-signed copies) or can be purchased internationally (unsigned) through the publishing website www.blurb.com.
